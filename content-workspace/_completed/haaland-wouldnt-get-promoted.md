## What the post argues

Erling Haaland fails every activity metric we use to evaluate engineers (about 24 touches per 90, a 9-touch full match) while being the deadliest finisher alive. Engineering promotion systems make the inverse mistake: they reward visible firefighting over the quiet work that prevents fires. The prescription: the business scoreboard exists, so do the attribution work of connecting quiet effort to it instead of counting whatever's easiest.
